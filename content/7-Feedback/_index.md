> Here, you can freely share your personal opinions about your experience participating in the First Cloud AI Journey program. This will help the FCAJ team improve any shortcomings based on the following aspects:

### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The working environment is exceptionally friendly, supportive, and open. FCAJ members are always willing to assist whenever I encounter technical difficulties or deployment blockers, even extending their support outside standard hours. The workspace is structured, tidy, and highly comfortable, which allowed me to stay deeply focused on my tasks. To make the journey even more memorable, incorporating more informal social gatherings or team-bonding activities would be a fantastic way to strengthen peer relationships.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The mentors provide highly detailed guidance, offering clear architectural explanations whenever I encounter ambiguous concepts while consistently encouraging active technical inquiry. The administration team handles all logistics smoothly, providing necessary documentation and establishing optimal conditions for an efficient workflow. I especially appreciate the mentorship style, which challenges me to independently debug and investigate infrastructure issues before stepping in with guidance, rather than just delivering immediate answers.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The engineering tasks assigned to me aligned perfectly with my university foundations in software development, while simultaneously expanding my horizon into advanced, cloud-native domains. Transitioning from standalone programming to provisioning real-world cloud infrastructure allowed me to reinforce my academic theories while absorbing highly valuable industry practices.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
Throughout this cloud journey, I acquired critical professional skills including agile project management workflows, collaborative Git governance protocols, and structured technical communication within an enterprise environment. Furthermore, the real-world experiences shared by the mentors provided me with excellent, data-driven insights to better plan my long-term career path as a Full-Stack Cloud Engineer.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The organizational culture is remarkably positive: everyone maintains mutual respect, approaches technical problems with extreme seriousness, yet preserves an enjoyable and welcoming atmosphere. During high-priority project sprints, the entire team naturally coalesced, offering cross-functional assistance regardless of hierarchy. This collaborative spirit truly made me feel like an integrated part of the engineering team rather than just an intern.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The program provides a highly motivating internship allowance and offers flexible working hours, which allowed me to manage my university obligations effectively. Additionally, gaining exclusive access to internal cloud engineering training sessions and mentor-led workshops stands out as a major benefit.

### Additional Questions
* **What did you find most satisfying during your internship?**  
    * The most rewarding experience was transitioning from drawing theoretical infrastructure designs on paper to actually building a production-ready **AI-powered English Scoring System**. Successfully integrating multi-tier AWS components (IAM, S3, SQS, Step Functions) and watching the automated state machine handle mock payloads to trigger external Generative AI APIs in real-time gave me immense practical confidence.
* **What do you think the company should improve for future interns?**  
    * While the structured learning path is excellent, the initial onboarding documentation regarding advanced enterprise boundaries (like hybrid subnet topologies and custom IAM condition keys) could be deepened. Providing a mini-troubleshooting handbook or common architectural error logs on day one would help future interns ramp up their debugging velocity faster.
* **If recommending to a friend, would you suggest they intern here? Why or why not?**  
    * Absolutely, I would highly recommend the First Cloud AI Journey program to any aspiring software or cloud engineer. Unlike traditional internships that limit students to trivial documentation tasks, FCAJ places interns at the forefront of modern technical challenges, forcing us to handle enterprise-level tooling, serverless topologies, and cutting-edge GenAI integrations under expert guidance.

### Suggestions & Expectations
* **Do you have any suggestions to improve the internship experience?**  
    * I highly suggest organizing a dedicated "Cross-Group Architecture Review" session mid-way through the program. Allowing different project teams to pitch their design choices, cost calculations, and structural bugs to one another would spark excellent peer learning and reveal diverse ways of applying the exact same AWS services.
* **Would you like to continue this program in the future?**  
    * Yes, I would definitely look forward to staying connected with the program, potentially transitioning into an alumni or mentor role for future cohorts to help incoming students navigate their initial cloud-native learning curves.
* **Any other comments (free sharing):**  
    * I want to extend my deepest gratitude to all the mentors, administrators, and my brilliant team members. This journey has completely reshaped my perspective on building scalable, full-stack systems and has given me a solid technical blueprint to confidently enter the enterprise engineering landscape.
